Meet Sharmin, Your Partner in Growth

Life Circle was founded on the belief that each child possesses a unique brilliance that just needs the right environment to flourish. As a counseling/developmental lead with over a decade of experience, I've seen first-hand the transformation that happens when professional guidance meets empathetic support.

Our methodology isn't about "fixing"—it's about understanding the unique neurological landscape of your child and building the bridges they need to reach their full potential.

"We don't just treat symptoms; we nurture the whole child and support the whole family."

Common Questions

What ages do you work with? expand_more

We primarily focus on children and adolescents from ages 3 to 18, providing developmentally appropriate care for each stage.

Do you offer remote sessions? expand_more

Yes, we offer high-quality teletherapy for many of our services, specifically training and emotional support sessions.

How long is the typical treatment plan? expand_more

Each child is different. Some families find success in 3-6 months, while others prefer ongoing support as the child hits new developmental milestones.
